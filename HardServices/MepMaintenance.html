PROJECT MANAGEMENT SERVICES

MEP Maintenance

R. is one of the leading MEP companies in Dubai, offering the MEP as part of its Facilities Management services. Our comprehensive MEP (mechanical, electrical and plumbing) maintenance capabilities are designed to give your assets uninterrupted and maximum performance through a schedule of planned preventative maintenance (PPM), routing breakdown callouts and emergency response services. We plan our maintenance programmes completely around managing your assets in the most efficient way. This includes assessing their effectiveness over time and making recommendations for replacement where applicable.

At every stage we apply the most stringent QHSE (quality, health, safety and environmental) controls in line with our international accreditations. Our on-site QHSE team regularly audits our site operations and remains vigilant to anything that may in any way compromise safety or affect the quality of our service delivery. This extends not only to INAYA’s own operations, but to any pre-approved specialist subcontractors we may deploy.

All our MEP and QHSE operations are managed through our CAFM (computer-aided facilities management) system, through which we can fully monitor our performance against the project-specific key performance indicators agreed between INAYA and the client. The CAFM system also provides clients with complete transparency on our performance deliverables through a ‘dashboard’ system which shows a complete status report on all aspects of our operations 24/7.
