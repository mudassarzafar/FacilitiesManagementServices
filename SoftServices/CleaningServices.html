PROJECT MANAGEMENT SERVICES

Cleaning Services

The scope of our cleaning services covers the high-level care and cleanliness of client facilities, internally and for external façades. At all times we strive to use the most environmentally-friendly, chemical-free cleaning products and equipment that reduces energy-usage.

Resourced to take on cleaning contracts for entire residential/commercial buildings and retail complexes, our skilled cleaning teams are trained to keep your facility in prime condition, with minimal disturbance and absolute safety to tenants and visitors.

Our cleaning regime is both comprehensive and thorough. Prior to mobilising on-site, we put together a detailed cleaning plan encompassing every area and facet of our clients’ premises – from floor surfaces, furniture, skirtings, carpet trims, pots and plants, to lighting, doors and handles, glass surfaces, elevators, escalators and staircases. Our specialised cleaning teams keep your premises in pristine condition 24/7, 365 days a year.

We understand that we are our clients’ brand ambassadors and place a huge emphasis on this. At all times our on-site cleaning staff are courteous, well-presented and helpful to all community members they interact with.
